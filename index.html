Focus Areas

Research Interests

Hybrid Systems

Modeling, stability analysis, and simulation of hybrid dynamical systems combining continuous and discrete behaviors.

Robust Control & Observers

Design of robust controllers and observer systems with formal stability guarantees for uncertain and nonlinear systems.

Multi-Agent Systems

Coordination, communication, and control of autonomous networked systems and distributed agents.

Aerospace & Space Systems

Control and dynamics applications for aerospace vehicles, spacecraft, and space domain awareness.

Biological Modeling

Applying hybrid systems theory and control frameworks to biological and physiological system models.

Communication Networks

Stability and control of networked systems with delays, packet loss, and intermittent communications.
