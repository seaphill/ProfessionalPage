Teaching Philosophy

I aim to connect classroom theory directly to open research problems — students leave my courses having implemented the same modeling and control techniques used in current spacecraft autonomy research. Coursework emphasizes hands-on simulation, intuition-building through hybrid systems examples, and exposure to real aerospace case studies drawn from ongoing work at AFRL and UNM.
